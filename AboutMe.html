Hi! My name is Kyle Huang

Welcome to my portfolio that includes all of my projects and lab work that I have done. I am an electronics engineer who loves to play sports and video games. I was originally a mechanical engineer but found myself in the ESET program at CPP where I fell in love with the work and potential. As someone who is heavily interested in tech and loves to build his own computers, electronics engineering seemed to be the place for me. Throughout my educational career I found myself developing a particular fondness to control systems and software design. In terms of character, I find myself to be a very diligent and hard-working individual. I hold myself to a high standard and try to do my best in any work that I undertake.
